# Use Cases

## 1. Field Verification

Used by event volunteers, FTAs, practice field operators, and teams building offseason fields.

F3iducial detects: shifted field elements, incorrect tag placement, asymmetric layouts, damaged or moved structures, accumulated setup error.

The goal is not millimeter metrology — it is **practical autonomous reliability**.

## 2. Robot-to-Field Calibration

Sometimes the field cannot be fixed. At competitions, teams often need to adapt their robot to the field they are actually playing on.

F3iducial's manual drive and inspection mode lets teams:
- Drive around the competition field
- Inspect tag positions
- Compare expected vs actual geometry
- Characterize autonomous offsets
- Generate corrected alignment data
